# 🛸 Space Tribes: Parasite

An asynchronous 4-player dice-battle game for Dave, Brian, Joel & Chris. A hostile alien parasite possesses one crew member each round — 3 humans roll their dice against the growing monster, scores stay secret until the dramatic final reveal after round 8.

The entire game is one file: **`index.html`**. No build step, no server code.

- **[SETUP.md](SETUP.md)** — 5-minute walkthrough: free Firebase database + GitHub Pages hosting
- **[RULES.md](RULES.md)** — the crew manual: scoring, tokens, upgrades, and strategy

Open the hosted page on your phone, tap your character once, and your phone remembers you. Play a round a day or blitz a whole game in an evening.
