# Space Tribes: Parasite — Crew Manual (v5)

**3–6 players.** Everyone hosts the parasite **twice**, then the whole crew fights the thing you built. Open scores. One champion — unless it escapes.

**v5.1.1** — Fair-half hosts, forge levels, escalating dice, mandatory Feeding Time, shaped dice, a built-parasite finale, and an optional Player 3 Computer. Schema **v14**. See [CHANGELOG.md](CHANGELOG.md).

## Two-human playtest

Choose **New game → 3 players → Use basic Computer as Player 3**. Enter the two human names for Player 1 and Player 2. Player 3 is marked **CPU**, cannot be joined from a phone, and automatically shops, rolls, and locks. When Player 3 is the host, it also grows the parasite, chooses its color path and food, and submits both sides. The game remains the normal seven-round three-player game: six infection rounds followed by the boss.

## The shape of the game

| Players | Infection rounds | Boss round |
|---|---|---|
| 3 | 6 | round 7 |
| 4 | 8 | round 9 |
| 5 | 10 | round 11 |
| 6 | 12 | **round 13** |

Every round one player is the **parasite host**. Everyone hosts exactly once in the first half and once in the second half. Everyone does **Shop → Roll → Lock in** on their phone. The crew's totals combine against the parasite host. **Ties go to the crew.**

## Energy ⚡ and reroll tokens 🎲

- **+6⚡ every round**, plus your banked **Base Energy**, plus **+2⚡ underdog** if you're in last place. Unspent banks.
- **🎲 reroll tokens**: you start every round with **1** (more from Base Reroll). Tap one human Attack die and reroll only that selected die; Mod and Wound reroll their section. A reroll **always spends a token first** — only at 🎲0 does it cost 1⚡.
- **Reroll all = always 2⚡.** Tokens never apply.

### The shop

| Buy | Cost | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| **Forge** | 1⚡ | Raise one face from L1 → L2 (+2) → L3 (+4 max) |
| **Mod upgrade** | 2⚡ | Raise a Mod die face one tier (max 3) — raises its **per-lock** gain |
| **Size up** | 3⚡ | Rebuild d6 → d8 → d10 with clean faces; all forges on that die are erased |
| **Extra die** | 4 / 5 / 6⚡ | Your first, second, and third extra Attack die (max 2 per color) |
| **Heal Wound** | 3⚡ | Remove one Wound die from your pool |

The phone shows the sizes as lightweight faceted shapes: d6 square, d8 diamond, d10 pointed pentagonal, and parasite d12 hexagonal. Forge badges show **L2** and **L3** directly on upgraded faces.

## Mod die — lock to add forever

Six faces: **Base Attack · Base Energy · Base Reroll · Base % · Points · Wound.**
During the roll, all six Mod sides appear beneath the die with that player's current upgraded values and levels; the rolled side is highlighted to support the reroll decision.

**Every time you lock a claim face, its value is added permanently. No cap.**

| Face | Per-lock gain (tier 1/2/3) | Stacks into |
|---|---|---|
| Base Attack | +3 / +4 / +5 | Every combat total, forever |
| Base Energy | +2 / +3 / +4⚡ | Your round stipend, forever |
| Base Reroll | +2 / +3 / +4🎲 | Your token base each round |
| Base % | +10 / +15 / +20% | Permanent % of your attack |
| Points | +3 / +4 / +5 | One-shot personal score — no combat |
| Wound | — | Lock it → grab a Wound die |

## Wound dice

One blank, five hurts: **−2⚡** · **subtract highest die** · **−50% of your Weapons / Strength / Agility**. Heal 3⚡. Cap 6. Reroll your Wound section like any other.

Wounds come from: a host choosing to hurt you for power · locking your own Mod Wound face.

## The host turn

1. **Grow the parasite.** It has its own ⚡ (stipend 7 at 5–6p, plus what past hosts banked). You must choose at least one upgrade: extra dice cost 4/5/6⚡ (max 4 total) · size-up 3⚡ (cap **d12**, erases that die's forge levels) · forge 1⚡ (L3 max). Every purchase is permanent. Leftover pays parasite rerolls, then banks.
2. **Choose your color attack path before rolling any dice:**

| Path | Tonight |
|---|---|
| 🔴 **Red** | Add **2.5× your Weapons total** |
| 🔵 **Blue** | Add **1.5× your Strength total** and gain **3 free parasite section rerolls** for Attack or Specialty |
| 🟢 **Green** | Add **1.5× your Agility total** and cancel the crew's **highest die** |

3. **Upgrade your human dice (optional).** Spend human Energy on forging, size-ups, Mod faces, healing, or an extra die.
4. **Continue to rolls and roll your human dice.**
5. **Run the parasite attack checklist:** **1)** Choose Your Food — select one or two eligible friends for one Wound and +3 parasite attack each. **2)** Roll Parasite Dice. **3)** Reroll or Keep — tap one parasite Attack die to highlight it, then reroll only that selected die; Specialty rerolls its own section; Reroll All changes the complete parasite package. Blue's free rerolls can pay for a selected Attack die or Specialty reroll, and unused rerolls expire. **4)** Lock Parasite Attack. A max Attack face **CRITS**: roll one bonus die and add it. The bonus roll does not chain another CRIT in the normal game.
   All six Specialty sides appear beneath the Specialty die. The rolled side is highlighted, and every side can be tapped for its full explanation before deciding whether to reroll. The six effects are: flat **+8**; **+25%** of Attack dice rounded down; add the **lowest Attack die** again; add the host's **highest human Attack die**; **blank**; or roll one **extra hot d8 Attack die**. The extra die can CRIT once just like a normal parasite Attack die.
6. **Read the attack receipt before locking.** It shows Growth Attack, every Attack die (including `max + CRIT bonus = die total`), the exact Specialty calculation, color-path bonus, Feeding Time bonus, parasite total, and combined host total.
6. **Lock your human roll and submit both sides.**

### Parasite Growth Attack

Parasite Growth Attack tracks the crew: roughly 45–47% of the previous round's total crew score, adjusted for table size. It hardens whenever the crew holds. It grows because you grow.

## Scoring

Crew holds (host gets nothing). Every winning crew player also gets **+2⚡ winner Energy**:

| | First half | Second half |
|---|---|---|
| Every crew member | 6 | 8 |
| Attack MVP — top 3 crew totals | +4 / +3 / +2 | +5 / +4 / +3 |
| Category (best single die per color, crew only) | +2 (+1⚡) | +3 (+1⚡) |

Parasite wins: the host receives **4 points** first half / **5 points** second half, plus **+4⚡ winner Energy**.

**Well-Rounded Bonus:** any player who locks at least one **6+ Weapons**, **6+ Strength**, and **6+ Agility** die in the same round earns **+2 points**, regardless of which side wins.

## The boss

The final target has three visible parts: the crew-strength mirror, the expected CRIT power of the parasite Attack dice every host built, and danger from Wounds plus host wins. Every forge, size-up, and extra parasite die now returns in the finale. The round log records who Wounded whom, so selfish host turns remain visible and can inspire retaliation. In the final round the whole table attacks together. Clear the target → hero ladder (12 down to 1) and **+5 kill blow** for the top total. Fail → the parasite escapes; consolation ranks only.

Current six-player real-engine calibration: 1,500 automated games, 53.7% host wins and 50.5% boss clears. Table playtesting still decides whether those outcomes feel dramatic rather than merely balanced.

## House rules worth trying

- **Chain-crits:** a CRIT's bonus roll can crit again. Chaos night only.
- **The Loser Hosts:** whoever finishes last buys wings.
